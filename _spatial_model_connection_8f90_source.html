18  !> Class to manage spatial connection of a model to one
19  !! or more models of the same type. Spatial connection here
20  !! means that the model domains (spatial discretization) are
21  !! adjacent and connected via DisConnExchangeType object(s).
22  !! The connection itself is a Numerical Exchange as well,
23  !! and part of a Numerical Solution providing the amat and rhs
24  !< values for the exchange.
